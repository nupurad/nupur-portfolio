Publications
An Efficient IoT-Blockchain Sharding Technique using Frequently Transacting Sender and Receiver Information
June 2024

Presented a blockchain sharding technique for IoT applications that groups frequently transacting sender-receiver pairs to reduce inter-shard communication and improve efficiency. Experimental results on the SocialChain blockchain show about a 10% execution time reduction and sustained TPS improvements, highlighting its scalability benefits.

Designing a Machine Learning-Based Intelligent Indoor Navigation System for people with Physical Disabilities
December 2023

Developed a machine learning-based intelligent indoor navigation system specifically designed to aid individuals with physical disabilities in complex indoor environments. Leveraged computer vision and natural language processing to provide personalized, accessible routes and voice-guided navigation, enhancing user independence and mobility.

Artificial Intelligence Application in Personalized Fintech
October 2023

Researched into integrating artificial intelligence into personalized fintech to enhance personal finance management. Proposed the use of machine learning, deep learning, and cognitive computing to offer features like expense tracking, fraud detection, and tailored investment recommendations while prioritizing data privacy and customer-centric design.

[Blog] Automatic Essay Scoring Systems
October 2023

Reviewed the evolution and methodologies of Automatic Essay Scoring systems, which aim to address the challenges of manual essay evaluation, such as bias and inefficiency. AES systems leverage advanced technologies like transformer models (e.g., BERT, RoBERTa), feature extraction techniques, word embeddings and LSTM architectures to assess essays based on multiple factors.
